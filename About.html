  • HISTORY

The eminent philanthropist and entrepreneur Sri Gopal Narayan Singh established the Narayan Group of Institutions under the aegis of Deo Mangal Memorial Trust, founded in 2000. The group has already curved its own niche in the field of education through the establishment of Gopal Narayan Singh University, Narayan Medical College & Hospital, Nursing College, Management College, Pharmacy College and Law College, just to mention a few.


Narayan World School emerged as the youngest member of the Narayan Group of Institutions under the umbrella of Shail Foundation, a sister organization of Deo Mangal Memorial Trust, in the year 2017.


  • About Us

With a group of well qualified and trained faculties Narayan World School focuses on the holistic development of the 200+ students catering to the individual needs of each student. Child centric modern pedagogical techniques are followed, based on Multiple Intelligence Theory and child psychology. Students are exposed to hands on experience and field study besides audio-visual aids, wherever and whenever required. Teachers. as foster parents, take special care so that the pupils can excel not only in academics but in co-curricular activities like sports, art and music as well, ultimately helping them to stand tall in their future life and contribute in the development of the nation and society.
